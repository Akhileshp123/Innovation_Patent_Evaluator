Title: Decentralized Autonomous Data Validation Engine (DAVE)

Abstract: A novel decentralized autonomous data validation engine (DAVE) utilizing a non-fungible token (NFT) based proof-of-stake mechanism to authenticate and certify the veracity of large-scale industrial IoT data streams. The core of the invention is a dynamic consensus protocol that adjusts validation node rewards based on historical accuracy and latency, thereby eliminating single points of failure common in centralized auditing systems. The system integrates a blockchain authenticated ledger for immutable logging and a personalized digital twin for simulating validation scenarios, dramatically improving data conservation metrics across diverse enterprise environments.
